Teriyaki Chicken

This is going to be my favorite recipe.

List of Ingredients:

  • 2 lb chicken thighs, sliced into chunks
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup of brown sugar

Steps:

  1. Sear the chicken thigs evenly in pan, then flip
  2. Add the soy sauce and brown sugar, stirring and bringin to a boil
  3. Stir until the sauce has reduced and evenly glazed the chicken
  4. Serve with rice, if desired!
  5. Enjoy!
